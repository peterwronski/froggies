Services

We cater for

  • Private Fine Dining either at your home or rented location for groups of 2 – 25 guests
  • Finger-type or Cold Buffets for up to 100 guests
  • Cooking lessons in the privacy of your own home
  • Consultancy

How it works

  • Get in touch with either Dominique or Didier , and we will meet with you to discuss all aspects of your requirements (including budget and wine choices to complement your menu and any allergies)
  • We buy the ingredients following confirmation
  • We come to your home to cook and serve
  • We tidy up!

Our prices are bespoke for each event, depending on your requirements and budget. Feel free to Contact Us to get a personalised quote.

Our Background

Didier and Dominque first met while completing their National Service in the French Navy (early 1980’s). Several years later, they both met again in Aberdeen ….could this be fate?

Both went on to successfully establish two of the most highly regarded restaurants on the Aberdeen “Foodie” map and beyond: The Silver Darling and Café Boheme. Following many years of running both businesses, they decided a change of pace was needed and the idea of “Froggies” was born.


Didier and Dominique have both won the following awards:

  • City Restaurant of the Year (Scotland)
  • Top 10 Seafood Restaurant (Sunday Times)
  • Grampian Restaurant of the Year
  • ...along with many others
